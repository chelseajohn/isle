Detailed Description

Represents a spacetime lattice.

Holds topology and geometry of the spatial lattice. Here; 'site' and 'neighbor' refer to sites on the spatial lattice.

Performs consistency checks on all inputs unless ndebug == true. Throws an instance of std::out_of_range or std::invalid_argument if a check fails.
